# Comprehensive 50-Year Fraktion Collaboration Analysis

## German Bundestag WP7-21 (1972-2025)

**Analysis Date:** December 16, 2025  
**Data Source:** vorgang_initiative table (joint fraktion initiatives on parliamentary procedures)  
**Temporal Coverage:** 50 years across 15 wahlperioden

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines how German parliamentary fraktionen (party groups) have collaborated on joint initiatives over five decades. Using the `vorgang_initiative` table, we tracked when multiple fraktionen jointly initiated parliamentary procedures (vorgänge).

### Key Discoveries

1. **Explosive Growth**: Fraktion collaboration increased **46-fold** from the 1970s to today

   - 1970s-1980s: 499 joint initiatives
   - 2017-2025: 23,105 joint initiatives

2. **Opposition Dominates Collaboration**: 64% of all joint initiatives are cross-party/opposition, only 36% involve coalition partners

3. **Most Collaborative Pair**: CDU/CSU + SPD (7,502 joint vorgänge, 20.2% of all collaborations)

4. **Pre-Coalition Building**: The "Ampel" coalition (SPD/Grüne/FDP) conducted 188 three-way initiatives in WP20 while in opposition, laying groundwork for their WP21 coalition

5. **AfD Pattern**: Unlike drucksachen (where AfD is isolated), vorgang initiatives show AfD collaborating with all parties (611 with CDU/CSU, 587 with SPD)

| FDP            | 344            | 15.0%                   |
| Die Linke      | 338            | 14.7%                   |

### Why the Difference from Drucksachen?

- **Vorgang initiatives** include procedural matters (Kleine Anfragen, committee work) where all parties participate
- **Drucksachen** (document authorship) represent voluntary policy collaboration - here AfD is isolated (only 1 bilateral)
- This suggests AfD is **procedurally included but politically isolated**

### Revolutionary Finding

**64% of all fraktion collaboration is cross-party/opposition, not between coalition partners.**

This reveals:

1. **Opposition uses joint initiatives strategically** to challenge the government
2. **Coalition partners collaborate through government machinery** (not parliamentary initiatives)
3. **Joint initiatives are tools of opposition, not governance**

## Conclusion: Key Takeaways

1. **Explosive Growth**: Fraktion collaboration increased 46-fold over 50 years (499 → 23,105 joint initiatives)

2. **Opposition Dominates**: 64% of collaborations are cross-party/opposition, revealing joint initiatives as tools of parliamentary oversight rather than governance

3. **Historical Patterns**:

   - 1972-1998: CDU/CSU + FDP dominated (Kohl era)
   - 1998-2009: Grüne + SPD alliance (red-green coalition)
   - 2009-2025: CDU/CSU + SPD (grand coalitions)
   - 2017-2025: Ampel triangle (SPD + Grüne + FDP) building coalition

4. **Pre-Coalition Building**: The Ampel coalition conducted 188 three-way initiatives in WP20 while in opposition, demonstrating strategic coalition preparation

5. **AfD Paradox**: AfD participates in procedural vorgang initiatives (611-587 with major parties) but is isolated in policy drucksachen (only 1 bilateral) - **procedurally included, politically isolated**

6. **Collaboration Types**: 60% are questions (Kleine/Große Anfragen), showing joint initiatives primarily serve parliamentary oversight function

7. **Most Collaborative Pairs**:
   - All-time: CDU/CSU + SPD (7,502, 20.2%)
   - Red-Green era: Grüne + SPD (3,790)
   - Kohl era: CDU/CSU + FDP (2,790)
   - Recent: Ampel triangle (2,153-2,391 per pair)
