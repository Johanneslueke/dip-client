# Person-Level Participation Tracking Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis tracks individual parliamentarian activity patterns across 858,218 aktivitäten (WP18-21, 2014-2025) involving 2,398 unique individuals. The data reveals **extreme activity concentration**, with government officials monopolizing response duties (98-100% specialists) while opposition members drive parliamentary questioning. Individual behavior patterns validate earlier fraktion-level findings: the FDP shows highest individual engagement (4,788 aktivitäten by top member), AfD demonstrates coordinated procedural obstruction, and coalition SPD/Greens/FDP members effectively abandon questioning roles when in government.

### Key Findings

1. **Government Specialization**: Parliamentary State Secretaries operate as 100% specialists in Antwort duties (789 responses from top 10 officials, zero other activities)
2. **FDP Hyperactivity**: Dr. Marcus Faber leads with 4,788 aktivitäten (WP19), 76% Kleine Anfrage focused
3. **Opposition Dominance**: Die Linke shows 82% question rates in opposition, dropping to 68% in fraktion-Gruppe split
4. **Career Longevity**: 30 individuals active across all 4 wahlperioden (WP18-21), spanning 11.2 years
5. **Activity Inequality**: Fraktion members show activity equality ratios of 0.26-0.48, indicating extreme concentration where top members do 2-4x average work

## Methodological Notes

### Data Sources

1. **Aktivitaet table**: 858,218 records (WP18-21, 2014-2025)
2. **Person table**: 5,811 persons total, 2,398 active in aktivitaet data
3. **Person_role table**: 2,733 role records (MdB, MdBR, ministers, etc.)
4. **Drucksache_autor_anzeige**: 114,876 formal author linkages (WP7-21, 50 years)

### Parsing Approach

- **Aktivitaet.titel parsing**: Extract person names and fraktionen from string format "Name, MdB, Fraktion"
- **Fraktion extraction**: LIKE pattern matching for AfD, Die Linke, Grüne, SPD, CDU/CSU, FDP
- **Name ambiguities**: Some duplicates possible (Dr., von, title variations)

### Coverage Limitations

- **12-year window**: Aktivitaet data limited to WP18-21 (2014-2025)
- **Historical context**: Drucksache_autor_anzeige provides 50-year complement but different data structure
- **Missing data**: Early wahlperioden (WP7-17) lack aktivitaet detail

### Validation

- **RQ7 consistency**: Jan Wenzel Schmidt counts match (121 vs 126, 4% variance acceptable)
- **RQ9 consistency**: Government question counts confirm zero-filing finding
- **Internal consistency**: Fraktion totals match person-level aggregations
